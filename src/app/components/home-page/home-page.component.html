About C3

Who We Are!

C3 is the college and young adult group dedicated to educating, equipping, and training godly leaders to go out into the world as Jesus’ disciples.

Our vision is to see this generation of college students & young adults transformed by the power of the gospel and to build up a close-knit community of believers ​that will grow and mature in faith.
